Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a lost item?

Simply fill out the report form with the item details, and we will notify you if it's found.

Can I upload a photo of the item?

Yes! You can upload an image of the item when filling out the report form.

What should I do if I find an item?

Please report it through the "Report a Found Item" section on our platform.

How will I be notified if my item is found?

You will receive an email or phone call based on the contact details you provided.
